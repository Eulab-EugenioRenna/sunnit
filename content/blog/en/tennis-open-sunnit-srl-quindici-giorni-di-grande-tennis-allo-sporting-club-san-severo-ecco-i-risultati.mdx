The **Open Sunnit SRL** has concluded with great success, an event that animated the courts of the Sporting Club San Severo for fifteen days, recording the participation of over 90 athletes and the playing of approximately 100 matches. The competition, which featured a total prize pool of 5,500 euros, attracted players from various Italian regions, confirming the growing prestige of the tournament within the national tennis scene.

The final day of competition took place on Sunday, June 7th, with the concluding acts for all scheduled categories. Despite the high afternoon temperatures, numerous enthusiasts filled the club's stands to watch the finals and support the event's protagonists, helping to create an audience setting worthy of major occasions.

## Final Results

### Men's Main Draw
The final match of the event, for the awarding of the prize money, saw the number one seed **Fabrizio Osti** (Tennis Club Napoli) face off against **Davide Liberati** (Avezzano). Although not starting as a favorite, Liberati played a tournament of absolute value. However, Fabrizio Osti made his experience count, prevailing with a score of **6-4 6-2**, claiming the Open Sunnit SRL title.

### Men's Fourth Category
Sporting Club San Severo's own **Gianluigi Olivieri** won the title by defeating his club mate Nicola Novelli with a score of **6-0 3-0 ret**.

### Men's Third Category
**Daniele Papeo** (Tennis Club Barletta) prevailed over Alberto Mastrangelo (Tennis Club Termoli) with a result of **6-3 6-3**, confirming the value displayed throughout the tournament.

### Women's Main Draw
**Beatrice Veltri** (Libero Tennis Cosenza) won the title by defeating Chiara Capurso (Circolo Tennis Fasano) with a score of **6-2 6-0**, in a final that highlighted the high technical level of the protagonists.

## Statements

The manager of Sunnit SRL, **Dante Marotta**, expressed his satisfaction:

> "We are very happy with how the tournament went and the overall success of the entire event. The event represented an extraordinary opportunity for visibility for our corporate brand, also thanks to the effective communication work carried out in collaboration with the Sporting Club San Severo.
>
> I would also like to praise the organizational capacity of the club and the entire city of San Severo. Being originally from San Severo, I am very happy that my city has demonstrated, once again, that it is welcoming, capable of hosting significant sporting events and transforming them into an opportunity for growth and promotion for the entire community."

Words of satisfaction also came from the **sports director of the Sporting Club San Severo**:

> "The technical level of the tournament was very high, both in the women's and men's draws. The members and enthusiasts who followed the event had the opportunity to witness matches of great quality and high-level tennis, with matches often fought until the very last game. The success of the event is also the result of the great work carried out by the club staff and everyone who contributed to the organization of an event that received praise from the athletes and professionals."

## Upcoming Events

With the Open Sunnit SRL successfully concluded, the Sporting Club San Severo prepares to host the final tournament of the spring-summer season: the **fourth category pre-qualifications for IBI2027**.

This competition will allow the best players of the IV category in the province of Foggia to gain access to the regional phases and continue to pursue the dream of reaching the prestigious courts of the Foro Italico. A new appointment that confirms the club's willingness to invest in the promotion of tennis and offer high-quality sporting events to the territory.
