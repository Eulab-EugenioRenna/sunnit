# Making digital design accessible

Accessibility improves the experience for everyone, not only for a subset of users.

## Begin with contrast

Readable text and clear interaction states reduce mistakes and hesitation.

## Use semantic HTML

Meaningful structure helps assistive technologies and improves overall content clarity.

## Do not leave it to the end

When accessibility is part of the project early, it costs less and delivers better outcomes.

Inclusive products are usually clearer, stronger, and easier to maintain.
