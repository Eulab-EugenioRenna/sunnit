# 10 principles for effective web design

Strong digital experiences come from consistent decisions, not from a single visual trick.

## Start with hierarchy

Headings, spacing, and contrast should tell users what matters first.

## Remove noise

Every unnecessary block slows reading and makes the right action harder to spot.

## Treat performance as part of design

A beautiful page that loads slowly still damages trust and conversion.

Good design is a balance of clarity, speed, and intent.
