Sporting Club San Severo is preparing for two weeks of great tennis with a prestigious national event that will bring athletes, enthusiasts, and professionals from all over Italy to the city of San Severo.

From May 25th to June 7th, at the historic club on Via Foggia, an important Men's and Women's Open tournament will take place, included in the national calendar, with a prestigious total prize pool of 5,500 euros and hospitality reserved for the highest-ranked players.

The event begins on May 25th, with the first week dedicated to qualifiers and basic categories, involving emerging competitors and the many enthusiasts who animate the local tennis movement. The tournament will then hit its stride in the second week, and specifically during the final five days of the event, when athletes of the highest technical profile will compete on the Sporting Club courts.

The competition will kick off on Monday the 25th. Currently, the provisional men's entry list features Francesco Bahamonte Nicastro (ranking 2.2) as the number one seed, an Italian-Argentine coming from Sporting Club Bisceglie 2.0. Among the expected protagonists are also Francesco Colasanto, Andrea Pacello, and Giuseppe Tresca, all with a federal ranking of 2.3 and considered among the main candidates for final victory.

As for the women's draw, the candidates for final victory include Federica Papa (ranking 2.6) and Chiara Capurso (2.7). Cristiana Totaro (2.8), a former under-14 regional champion who trains steadily at Sporting Club San Severo, can be considered the right outsider; the young athlete, in addition to having improved technically, will enjoy all the warmth of the San Severo club, where she trains with commitment and dedication every day.

For this reason, the event is of great relevance not only for Sporting Club San Severo but for the entire city. For over two weeks, the center of the Alto Tavoliere will experience a significant movement of players, accompanying coaches, and enthusiasts, with positive economic and image impacts for the territory.

The event represents a high-profile appointment. Accommodation facilities, commercial activities, and city services will be involved in welcoming guests, once again offering an authentic image of Southern Italian hospitality and confirming San Severo as a sporting and organizational reference point in the regional and national tennis landscape.

The main sponsor of the event will be SUNNIT SRL, an entrepreneurial entity operating in the development of integrated artificial intelligence systems to support professional activities and advanced consulting networks. The company has operational offices in Rome, Valencia, Padua, and Milan and represents one of the emerging excellences in the sector of technological innovation applied to professional and commercial services and reserved integrated consulting networks.

As the sole and main partner, SUNNIT SRL will support the organization of the event. The two weeks of the tournament will also be enriched by a series of collateral events, cultural initiatives, and promotional moments aimed not only at the club members but at the entire citizenship and all enthusiasts taking part in the event. The goal will be to make the reality of Sporting Club San Severo and the innovative project carried forward by SUNNIT SRL more widely known, creating a bridge between sport, territory, culture, and technological innovation.

The two weeks of the tournament represent the start of a valuable collaboration that begins with the first edition of the Open Tournament – SUNNIT SRL Trophy, intended to represent only the first step of a common path looking to the future with ambition, in the perspective of consolidating over the years an increasingly important event for the sporting and social landscape of the city of San Severo and the entire territory.

Thus, a precious synergy is born. “I would first like to express my heartfelt gratitude to our main sponsor, SUNNIT SRL, and in particular to Dr. Dante Marotta, a San Severo native who chose to enthusiastically support this sporting project and the growth of our city through a national-level event,” declares Antonello Del Sordo – sporting director of Sporting Club San Severo.

“I also thank the members of Sporting Club San Severo, who I am sure will fill the stands during the two weeks of the tournament, the collaborators, the enthusiasts, and all those who wish to get closer to this great sporting event.”

“Tennis today represents one of the driving sports of the amateur movement across the entire national landscape. The extraordinary victories of the Italian champions are bringing more and more young people and families to this discipline, creating enthusiasm, participation, and a new sporting culture. For us, it will be a matter of pride to host athletes of such a high level in San Severo and offer the city two weeks of sport, aggregation, and territorial promotion,” concludes the DS.

On weekdays, matches will start from 2:00 PM, while on holidays from 10:00 AM. Admission is free and open to all citizens.
