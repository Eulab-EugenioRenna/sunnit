For InfoCert Tinexta, a project was carried out dedicated to the development of solutions in the field of **Digital Trust**, with the aim of supporting secure, reliable, and trust-oriented digital processes.

The activities involved the evolution of functionalities and services related to:
* Digital identity
* Secure information management
* Validation of digital processes

The work contributed to strengthening the quality of the solutions, improving operational reliability and the value of the digital services offered to users and organizations.
