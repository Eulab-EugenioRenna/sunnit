For Generali, a database revision and reorganization project was carried out, aiming to improve the quality, structure, and usability of company information.

### Information Architecture Optimization
The intervention involved redesigning and optimizing the information architecture, making the data more organized, accessible, and consistent with operational needs. This approach allowed for:
* Creating a more reliable foundation for process management.
* Facilitating the development of new advanced features.

### Artificial Intelligence Integration
Within the project, components based on **artificial intelligence** were integrated, with the objective of:
* Further enhancing the value of the available information assets.
* Making analysis, research, and decision-support activities more effective.

### Results Achieved
The work performed contributed to making the system more efficient, robust, and ready for future evolution, improving both internal information management and the organization's ability to use it strategically.
