For CDP, an **AI-based POC** was developed with the aim of validating an innovative approach to the management and enhancement of corporate information.

### Objectives and Activities
The activity allowed for the experimentation of intelligent functionalities applied to:
* **Consultation** of content
* **Analysis** of data
* **Interpretation** of information

The main objective was to verify the effectiveness of these tools within a concrete corporate context.

### Results
The final result is a **functional prototype** that demonstrates the potential of artificial intelligence as a support for internal processes, laying the groundwork for possible future developments.
