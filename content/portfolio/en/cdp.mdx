For CDP, a project focused on **artificial intelligence applied to document management** was developed, with the aim of making the consultation, analysis, and organization of large quantities of documents more efficient.

The work allowed the document management to be transformed from a simple information archive into an **evolved operational support tool**. Through intelligent functionalities, users can:

* Locate relevant information more quickly.
* Query content in a more natural way.
* Obtain advanced support in document analysis.

This intervention introduced a more modern approach to information consumption, drastically reducing search times and improving the ability to extract value from available content.

The final result is a more effective, accessible, and productivity-oriented document system, capable of better supporting internal activities and simplifying processes that previously required longer times and greater manual intervention.
